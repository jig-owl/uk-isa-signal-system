UK ISA Signal Dashboard

Metrics Explanation:

  • Signal: BUY = upward momentum, SELL = downward, HOLD = neutral
  • Trend: Bullish = uptrend, Bearish = downtrend
  • RSI: Relative Strength Index (0–100); below 30 = oversold, above 70 = overbought
  • Price: Current market price in stock's currency
  • Position Size: Suggested trade size based on your capital
  • Risk per Trade: Maximum loss you are willing to take
  • Stop Price: Price to exit if trade goes against you
